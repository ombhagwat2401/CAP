Our Work

Rescue Operations

CAP Mission, Vision & Values

Our mission is to build a compassionate world for animals in need through ethical rescue, care, and education where we envision a harmonious world where animals are revered with dignity, their rights and freedoms honoured, and they coexist in harmony with humans and nature. We value integrity, transparency and accountability in our work with teamwork, collaboration and diversity in our organization. with innovation, creativity and excellence in our services, we value compassion, empathy and kindness in our interactions for animals in need.
