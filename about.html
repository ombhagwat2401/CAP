Founder, Citizens for Animal Protection Foundation

Dear Friends, I am thrilled to welcome you to the website of Citizens for Animal Protection Foundation (CAP), an organization dedicated to promoting the welfare of animals in Maharashtra, India. As the founder of CAP, I want to take a moment to share with you the pressing need, current situation, and our solution-oriented approach towards animal welfare. Animals play a vital role in our lives, whether as companions, service animals, or as essential components of our ecosystems. However, despite their significance, animals often find themselves in vulnerable and neglected conditions. The situation for animals in Maharashtra is no exception. Many animals suffer from abuse, neglect, abandonment, and lack of proper healthcare. The ever-growing population, rapid urbanization, and inadequate awareness contribute to their plight. At CAP, we believe that animals deserve love, compassion, and care. We strive to provide them with a voice, advocate for their rights, and create a society where their well-being is prioritized. Our foundation is built on the pillars of empathy, education, and action, focusing on three key areas divided into different avenues of animal welfare and rights : Warm regards, Sushank Tomar Founder, Citizens for Animal Protection Foundation
